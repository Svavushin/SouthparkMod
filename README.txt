Recommended formats:
- Sound: WAV, 44.1 kHz, short clip. OGG may also work, but WAV is the safest first try.
- Kenny apparel images: PNG with transparency. The east texture is mirrored for west by RimWorld.
- JesusResurrection.png: PNG with transparency, 512x512 or 1024x1024.

The mod intentionally does not include South Park audio or images. Supply your own local fan-use assets.
